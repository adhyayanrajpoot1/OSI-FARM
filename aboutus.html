About Us

Our History

Established in 2017, OSI farminf Pvt. Ltd. is a globally renowned manufacturerof Farm Equipment under the brand name OSI FARM. Synonymous with quality and durability, OSI FARM is a brand of choice amongst farmers in India Disc Ridgers (Bund Maker), Tyne Ridgers, Tipping Trailers, Spring Loaded Tillers, Power Harrow, Ripper, Box Blade, Terracer Blade, Rotor Seed Drill, Sugar Cane Loader, Rigid Tillers, Land Levellers, Post Hole Diggers, Sub Soilers, Rotary Slashers, Reciprocating Forage Mower, etc. Marked by 38 years of experience, we have been contributing to agricultural growth by providing innovative implements at the most affordable prices.

Adhyayan Rajpoot Founder

The Founder and Path Breaker was entrepreneurship & vision personified. Out of nowhere he conceptualized the idea of quality agricultural equipment manufacturing in a scenario where nobody ever thought of role of quality, durability, precision in a product, which was subordinate to the prime mover (tractor). Taking such a challenge post retirement with his lifelong savings was as daring as it could be. His unshakable belief, focus & commitment to his ideology gradually but surely brought dignity & recognition to a trade which was considered to be road side activity. Company owes the present stature and glory to the continued commitment and unwavering passion of our founder

Prakhar Raj Managing Director

Mr. Prakhar Raj is the Managing Director of OSI FARMS. An entrepreneur at heart, he joined the business in the year 2017. He understood the market needs and transformed the company accordingly by establishing sustainable quality manufacturing, defining the vision for Research & Development while keeping emerging needs as the key focus. His vision and effort have resulted in OSI’s impressive success over the last 3 decades. His strong belief in customer delight and uncompromising business ethics has helped BUPL go global today while staying true to its values and promise. He is a tremendous source of inspiration for whole OSI family.

Abhijeet Satyadev Director

Mr. Abhijeet Satyadev joined OSI in November 2018 as the Director - Business Development. He holds a degree in Mechanical Engineering from University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign and an MBA in Marketing & Strategy from Indian School of Business. Prior to joining OSi he worked with Deloitte Consulting and Manufacturing Sector. He is responsible for defining domestic and international sales and marketing strategy and has been the force behind Fieldking’s expanding national and global footprint. He is a visionary strategist who is constantly motivating young talent for achieving new horizon of growth.

Armatya Kumar Director

Mr. Armatya Kumar Joined OSI in December 2017 as Director – Operations. He holds a Management degree- MBA in Finance & Operations from Department of Management Sciences, University of Pune (P.U.M.B.A). Before joining BUPL he has worked in consulting space with Avalon Global Research. He drives Strategic Initiatives in Operations & Finance vertical of OSI. He’s been instrumental in design and implementation of process framework and investments in automation and infrastructure development. He has set up a sustainable platform needed to support the fast paced growth of the company.
